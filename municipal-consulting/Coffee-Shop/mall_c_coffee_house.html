Design concept

Industry meets nature

Five 40-foot high-cube sea containers arranged side by side create a raw, industrial shell — then dissolved on the west face by a post-free bifold glass wall that makes the park the room.

🏗️
Sea container structure
Five 40 ft × 8 ft high-cube containers placed side by side on a concrete slab. The container corner castings carry a steel ridge frame spanning 75 ft for the peaked roof. No additional structural columns required inside.
🔲
Post-free bifold glass — west
Full 75 ft × 18 ft bifold glass wall facing the park. NanaWall SL73-type system — no corner posts, no structural columns in the opening. Eight leaves fold to side pockets; the entire front of the coffee house becomes open air in warm months.
❄️
Peaked standing-seam roof
4:12 pitch steel ridge frame on container corner castings. Standing-seam metal roofing sheds Cleveland snow loads. 3 ft overhangs front and rear. Clerestory glazing at ridge brings natural light deep into the dining floor.
☕
To-go bifold counter windows — east
Centered on the east wall, a compact to-go counter with bifold pass-through windows serves walk-up and street traffic. Solid container wall flanks the opening on both sides — a deliberate window-booth aesthetic.
🍳
Hood-equipped hot line
Full Type I commercial hood over 6-burner range, convection oven, and fryer. Upblast exhaust stack penetrates between containers 4 and 5 at the ridge valley. Makeup air unit in the ceiling plenum above the kitchen.

Bifold system detail

The wall disappears

When the panels fold, there is no threshold, no post, no column. The park becomes the dining room. Cleveland summer mornings, the coffee house is open air from door to door — 75 feet of nothing between you and the lawn.

①
Top track only
All structural load transfers to the container header beam — no posts in the opening. The bottom track is flush with the slab surface.
②
Panels fold to side pockets
Eight leaves stack into container-framed side pockets at each end. Fully recessed — panels are invisible when open.
③
12mm DGU solar control glazing
Double-glazed units with low-E coating. West exposure means afternoon sun in winter — critical to manage heat gain in summer when panels are closed.
④
East counter bifold — same system
The to-go counter on the east wall uses horizontal bifold counter windows on the same post-free principle — opens completely for warm weather, closes in winter while still serving through a smaller pass.

Project phases

Path to opening

From concept approval through certificate of occupancy — the phased delivery sequence for Mall C Coffee House.

Phase 01
Concept approval + permitting
FOS 216 finalizes floor plan, elevations, and specifications. Structural engineer stamps container and roof frame drawings. Building permit submission to Cleveland Division of Building & Housing. Health Department pre-application meeting.
Phase 02
Site prep + container procurement
Concrete slab poured with embedded anchors at container corner casting locations. Five 40 ft high-cube containers sourced, inspected, and delivered. Openings cut for bifold wall, to-go windows, service entry, and hallway on-site or at fab yard.
Phase 03
Steel + roof frame
Containers set and welded to anchor bolts. Steel ridge frame fabricated and erected. 4:12 standing-seam metal roof installed including ridge cap, gutters, and downspouts. Exhaust stack penetration for hood system roughed in.
Phase 04
MEP + bifold installation
Rough-in of electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and gas. Type I hood and upblast fan installed. NanaWall post-free bifold system installed west wall and east to-go counter. Walk-in cooler box set. Insulation and interior sheathing.
Phase 05
Interior finish + equipment
Flooring, interior cladding, lighting. Espresso equipment, commercial kitchen equipment, and service bar millwork installed. Restrooms finished and ADA clearances confirmed. Signage and exterior finish.
Phase 06
Inspections + certificate of occupancy
Final inspections: building, fire, health, electrical. Cleveland Health Department food service license issued. Staff training, soft open, and grand opening. Mall C Coffee House opens to the park.
