The Concept

Where the Midwest becomes the world's stage

The Midwest is the birthplace of house, techno, and rock — yet it has never had a world-class music industry event to call its own. No major music conference exists between SXSW and WMC. 22 million people live within a five-hour drive of Cleveland. This is the opportunity.

01
The Gap

No Flagship Event

Detroit techno. Chicago house. Ohio hip-hop. The Midwest invented electronic music culture — and has no destination event to honor or monetize it. That changes now.

02
The Venue

North Coast Village

Anchored by the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame, the Great Lakes Science Center, and the soon-to-be-redeveloped stadium site — the finest underdeveloped festival ground in America.

03
The Moment

Science Meets Sound

No other major event merges the music industry's most powerful conference with a lakefront science gathering. GLSMG Friday is unique in the festival landscape.

Venue Anchors

The North Coast Landmark Corridor

Three iconic institutions on one lakefront campus — each playing a distinct role in the MMC and GLSMG experience.

MMC Cultural Partner

Rock & Roll Hall of Fame

Existing lakefront landmark and the natural home for MMC's cultural programming. The opening night reception, Midwest legacy exhibitions, and the Wednesday evening host venue. A symbol of everything Cleveland has given the world musically.

GLSMG Friday Anchor

Great Lakes Science Center

The Science Center grounds host the GLSMG's Friday science expo, student showcases, and institutional keynotes. Partner institutions — CWRU, Cleveland State, Tri-C, and CMSD — activate their pavilions across the Science Center campus.

Future Development · Post-2030

Huntington Bank Ballpark Site

Home of the Cleveland Guardians until stadium removal in 2030. The cleared site creates a massive new event field adjacent to the Rock Hall and Science Center — doubling the available event footprint for MMC/GLSMG from Year 2 or 3 onward.

★ Stadium removed 2030 — Future event field + development opportunity
Competitive Advantage

Why Cleveland Wins

🗺️

22M People Within 5 Hours

The largest drive-in catchment area of any major US music market. Detroit, Pittsburgh, Columbus, Cincinnati, and Toronto all within reach.

🌊

Lake Erie Waterfront

Sunrise and sunset sets on the water — a visual and sonic signature rivals cannot copy. The closing fireworks over Lake Erie are a moment Ultra and WMC simply cannot replicate.

🏟️

Huntington Convention Center

1.8M sq ft convention facility for MMC — walkable from all North Coast Village sites. Attached directly to the Hilton Cleveland Downtown.

🏨

13,000 Hotel Rooms

Downtown Cleveland within walking distance of all venues. The hotel corridor along E. 4th and E. 9th streets fully supports peak-weekend demand.

☀️

Peak Summer Climate

Late July averages 82°F in Cleveland — ideal outdoor festival weather with a cooling lake breeze. Far more comfortable than Miami in March.

🎸

Midwest Cultural Heritage

Cleveland is the rock capital of America. Detroit techno and Chicago house are 90 minutes away. This event honors the music that actually built global culture.

Road to Inaugural

Development Timeline

From city presentation to inaugural event — a 24-month path to Cleveland's biggest annual event.

Q2 2026 — Now
City Presentation & MOU Discussion
Present to City Council, Department of Economic Development, and Port Authority. Begin MOU framework for 5-year initial term.
Q3 2026
Venue License Negotiation
North Coast Village exclusive use agreement, infrastructure capacity assessment, engineering review for power, water, and traffic management.
Q4 2026
Permitting & Infrastructure Planning
Noise ordinance variance, pyrotechnics approval, food vendor licensing, alcohol service coordination. CPD, CFD, and EMS liaison established.
Q1 2027
Partnership LOIs & Sponsor Commitments
Educational institution agreements signed. Presenting and platinum sponsor packages closed. Booking agency preferred vendor alignment.
Q2 2027
Artist Booking & Lineup Announcement
Headliner deals closed. MMC speaker roster announced. GLSMG science keynotes confirmed. Public lineup reveal and media launch.
Q3–Q4 2027
Ticketing Launch & Production Build
General admission and VIP sales open. MMC badge credentialing live. Site production build commences. Stage engineering and audio system installation.
July 2028 — Inaugural Event
MMC + GLSMG — First Annual Edition
Four days. 80,000 daily. The Midwest's greatest music event opens on the North Coast.
2030 — Major Expansion
